(Assume \( p(i), q(i)>0, \forall i) \).

Define
\[
    S=\frac{1}{n} \sum_{k=1}^{n} \frac{p\left(Y_{k}\right) h\left(Y_{k}\right)}{q\left(Y_{k}\right)}
